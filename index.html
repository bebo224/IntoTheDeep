THE HUMAN FOOTPRINT ON OUR OCEANS

THE OCEAN IS NOT A GARBAGE CAN

The ocean, covering over 70% of our planet, acts as Earth's lifeline, providing oxygen, regulating temperatures, and supporting countless species.

Yet, human activity has scarred its depths, leaving a legacy of pollution and neglect.

Every year, millions of tons of waste—plastics, chemicals, and debris—are dumped into the ocean, devastating marine life and threatening the delicate balance of this vital ecosystem.

The ocean, a vital lifeline for our planet, is now choking under the weight of human negligence.
